月亮升起，照在三棵聖誕樹上
今夜聖人將誕生在我們的心裡
為我們的罪
找到一個理由
赦免它
從此成為一個沒有罪的人
行義，守信，博愛
從此安心成為一個寂寞的人
安心走在路上
在塵土中，也沒有忘記
有一個嬰兒帶來的生命
帶來的死亡
就像那一支蠟燭的火光
使人明白黑暗光明
並沒有非常明確的界線
我們要在是非遲疑時
繼續行該行的路
～
聖誕節還很遙遠，感謝主耶穌
很久沒吃了鮭魚飯
彭教授送我真的好書
感謝范教授，送的植物園費南雪
～～~
The moon rises, casting light upon three Christmas trees.
Tonight, the Saint will be born in our hearts.
For our sins,
Find a reason,
Forgive it,
And henceforth become a person without sin.
Act justly, keep faith, be charitable,
And henceforth find peace in being a lonely soul.
Walk the path with ease,
In the dust, without forgetting,
There is a life brought by a Baby,
And death it brings,
Like the flame of a candle,
Making one understand the contrast of darkness and light.
There is no very clear boundary,
We must, in moments of hesitation,
Continue on the path we ought to tread.
